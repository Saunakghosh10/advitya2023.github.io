ECom-Thon

E Com-Thon will be a competition to bring forward ideas, entrepreneurship skills and peer learning amongst students. With three rounds, teams will be tested on their knowledge, presentations, pitching methods and innovative & practical ideas. The ideas could be from any domain of interest which involves software development. The flow of the event would be as follows: Round 1 will comprise of quiz, round 2 will allow pitching of ideas and the final round will have rebuttals and loophole searching. After the three rounds, three teams will be announced as winners. This will promote higher order thinking in students and get them industry level exposure along with the expert inputs they need.

Rules and Regulations:

  • Conducted by offline mode.
  • Participation: Team (3 members only).
  • Registration fee: 150/team.

TECHTRIX

TECHTRIX aims at educating and spreading awareness about various technologies with some interactive and fun events. It will have 4 rounds, 1st being the puzzle round, where puzzles like sudoku and crosswords will be solved. The 2nd round is a Pyramid Scheme round which is a team-based, fun word-guessing game. WIN, LOSE OR DRAW: the 3rd round is a Pictionary round that’ll require creativity and drawing skills to explain the secret phrase. The Final round: QUIZ ME THIS- QUIZ ME THAT, is a technical quiz where contestants have to answer a series of multiple-choice questions to advance to the next level/question within a given time. The difficulty of questions will be increased with each level. The team to hustle its way through all these rounds will be declared the winner. All participants will be awarded an e-certificate of participation and the top two winners will be given a Certificate of Appreciation and a prize

Rules and Regulations:

  • Conducted by offline mode.

Stock Overflow

Stock overflow is a Hands-on workshop on Stock Price Prediction using ML & DL Techniques where students will understand stock price prediction using data science techniques and be able to apply these techniques to a real-world problem. The incomplete information concerning the stock market data is often regarded as noisy characteristics, making it a challenge to predict the future price of a stock. Due to the rapid increase in trade and investment, the need for the appropriate tools and methods to mitigate risks and maximize gains equally increased.We have joining with us a very special guest – Mr. Diptanshu Kumar Sinha who is a quantitative Analyst at Rauch International Inc. This time he will be among us to upskill and help us through the plethora of experience which he carries with himself. 🎯 Students will get an overview of the techniques and tools such as data visualization, statistical analysis, and machine learning algorithms. 🎯 Students could analyze the stock market data and identify different trends or patterns. 🎯 Students would be able to use statistical methods to analyze the data and identify any trends or patterns that may be used to predict stock prices. 🎯 Students would be able to use data visualization tools to create charts and graphs to better understand the data and identify any correlations between different factors and stock prices. 🎯 The event would be concluded with an open book group activity wherein students would be provided a data set using which they need to visualize data. The group doing the best visualization would be appreciated with courses of Henry Harvin worth Rs.15000/-.

Rules and Regulations:

  • Conducted by offline mode.
  • Participation: Individual
  • Registration fee: 75/individual
