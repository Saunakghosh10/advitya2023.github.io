GameFlix

After all the days of coding, when the students get exhausted, they require some fun and leisure. This event is being conducted at Vellore Institute of Technology, Bhopal by the iOS Club. A 6-hour long fun and challenging series of games that consists of the following three rounds. Round 1: Money Heist → Are you ready for another heist? This time the Professor won’t help! You’ll be given a set of pictures, in which lies the hidden code to the vault. But beware- not all withhold the clue! Note: For this round, all you would need is your phone. Round 2: Squid Game → Life is tough but is certainly easier than the Squid Games. This time you won’t have to lick the back of the honeycomb to melt it. Every member shall be given a sheet of thermocol, and you’ll need to cut out perfect pieces of a specified object within a time limit. You don’t want to be shot by the Red Masked Men, do you? Round 3: Wednesday → Welcome to Nevermore Academy! Search for the letters of the word using the provided hints and a college map as you set out on a quest to discover who the monster is. But ensure stealth – don’t get eaten by an outcast.

Rules and Regulations:

  • Conducted by offline mode.
  • Participation: Team (4 members only)
  • Registration fee: 100/team.

ART RUN

Artful Treasure Hunt is an exciting event that combines the thrill of a treasure hunt with the creativity of painting. Participants form teams of three and set out on their task to seek the prize by completing various challenges which involve painting. At each challenge station, teams must solve puzzles or complete tasks of painting by using the given clue that leads them to the next station. The goal of Artful Treasure Hunt is to be the first team to reach the final destination where a surprise awaits them.
